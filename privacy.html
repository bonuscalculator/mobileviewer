Privacy Policy

We built MobileViewer to be private by design. Here's exactly what we collect, what we don't, and how we protect you.

Effective: January 1, 2025
Last updated: January 1, 2025

✦ The Short Version

MobileViewer runs entirely in your browser. We do not store URLs you preview, we do not track your browsing sessions, and we do not sell your data. Basic analytics (page views, country-level) may be collected anonymously to help us improve the tool.

1. Information We Collect

MobileViewer is a client-side web tool. The tool itself — the URL viewer and device simulator — operates entirely within your browser and does not transmit the URLs you enter to our servers.

We may collect the following limited, non-personal information:

  • Analytics data: Page views, session duration, browser type, operating system, and approximate geographic region (country-level). This data is aggregated and anonymized.
  • Error logs: If the application encounters an error, basic technical information (error type, browser version) may be logged to help us diagnose and fix issues.
  • Contact form data: If you contact us via our contact page, we collect your name, email address, and the content of your message.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

🛡 Privacy-First Design

The following data is never collected, logged, or transmitted by MobileViewer under any circumstances.

  • The URLs you enter into the MobileViewer tool
  • The content of websites you preview
  • Your browsing history within previewed websites
  • Login credentials or form inputs entered into previewed sites
  • Your IP address (beyond what hosting infrastructure logs anonymously)
  • Personal identifiers of any kind

3. How We Use Information

The limited anonymous information we may collect is used solely for:

  • Service improvement: Understanding which devices and features are most used so we can prioritize development.
  • Performance monitoring: Ensuring the application loads quickly and works correctly across different environments.
  • Support: Responding to messages sent through our contact form.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose beyond operating and improving MobileViewer.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

MobileViewer uses minimal browser storage:

  • No tracking cookies are set by MobileViewer itself.
  • Session preferences (such as your last-selected device) may be stored in your browser's localStorage for convenience. This data never leaves your device.
  • Analytics providers (if used) may set their own cookies. See the Third-Party Services section below.

You can clear any stored data at any time through your browser's developer tools or site settings.

5. Third-Party Services

MobileViewer may integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Vercel: Our hosting provider. Vercel's infrastructure may log basic request metadata such as timestamps and response codes. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts: Typography is loaded from Google Fonts, which may collect anonymized request data per Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Analytics: If we use an analytics service, it will be privacy-focused (such as Plausible or Fathom) which does not use cookies and does not collect personal data.

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any third party for marketing purposes.

6. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. MobileViewer uses HTTPS for all connections, ensuring data in transit is encrypted. Since the tool is client-side and we collect minimal data, the attack surface is inherently small.

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to avoid entering sensitive credentials or personal information into websites previewed through MobileViewer, as with any browser-based iframe tool.

7. Children's Privacy

MobileViewer is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability

Since MobileViewer collects minimal personal data, most rights requests will be straightforward to fulfill. Contact us at the address below to exercise any of these rights.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of MobileViewer after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
